Dataset Overview

This repository was established to centralize and store ecological data collected from urban gardens across Tokyo. These findings served as the foundational logic for an interactive ecosystem installation, developed as a Master’s research project within the Interface Design Lab (Department of System Design, Industrial Art course) at Tokyo Metropolitan University between 2024 and 2026.

The primary objective of this data collection was to identify and categorize biological parameters essential for structuring a virtual ecosystem. This dataset emphasizes variables that drive interactive simulation, such as: how species interact with their environment and each other; daily and seasonal periods of activity and dormancy; specific traits and "interesting aspects" of fauna survival within the constraints of Tokyo’s domestic green spaces.
